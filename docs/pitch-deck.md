# 10-Slide Pitch Deck

## 1. Problem

Creators lose margin and control when monetization lives inside closed platforms.

Visual:
One sentence over a screenshot of a creator publishing flow and a crowded stack of take-rate intermediaries.

Speaker note:
Digital monetization is still platform-first, not creator-first.

## 2. Current solutions are broken

- Gumroad and Patreon take a cut.
- Wallet-native payments still feel bolted on.
- Access is not portable after checkout.

Visual:
Three-column comparison: platform fee, payment speed, portability.

Speaker note:
Current tools either own the audience or make crypto UX feel ceremonial.

## 3. Our solution

A creator sets a price. A buyer pays in SOL. Content unlocks. Access can become a transferable NFT.

Visual:
Creator -> link -> buyer -> SOL -> unlocked content -> access pass.

Speaker note:
The product is deliberately small so the primitive is obvious.

LIVE DEMO

Visual:

Switch to the product and narrate the creator-to-buyer flow.

## 5. How it works

- React frontend for creation, payment, and unlock.
- Solana program for pricing and direct payment.
- Supabase for metadata, storage, and purchase persistence.
- Optional NFT access pass layered on top of the core paywall.

Visual:
Three-box system diagram.

Speaker note:
On-chain only holds what must be trusted. Everything else stays lean off-chain.

## 6. Market opportunity

- Creator economy: massive and still growing.
- Wallet-native payments: growing user behavior, not just infrastructure.
- Small take from creator monetization volume becomes a meaningful business.

Visual:
Two bars: creator economy and wallet payments, with a tiny highlighted slice.

Speaker note:
We do not need the whole market. We need the programmable layer inside it.

## 7. Why Solana

- Fast enough to demo live.
- Cheap enough for small digital purchases.
- Composable with wallets, NFTs, and future payment logic.

Visual:
Three proof points with confirmation time, fee profile, and ecosystem logos.

Speaker note:
This is a product argument for Solana, not a chain-maxi argument.

## 8. Why now

- Wallet UX is finally consumer-ready.
- Creators need monetization that travels with the open internet.
- Stablecoin and wallet behavior is moving from speculation to utility.

Visual:
Timeline showing wallet maturity, creator monetization pressure, and infrastructure readiness.

Speaker note:
The timing matters because the rails finally feel product-grade.
