# Demo Video Shot List

## Target

- Length: 60 to 90 seconds
- Format: 16:9 MP4
- Product visible in the first 5 seconds

## Shots

### Shot 1 — Problem hook (0:00-0:06)

- Screen: landing page hero
- Voiceover: creators should not lose margin and control just to charge for digital access

### Shot 2 — Creator setup (0:06-0:20)

- Screen: creator studio
- Actions:
  - enter title
  - set price
  - choose `Transferable access pass`
  - create monetized link

### Shot 3 — QR moment (0:20-0:30)

- Screen: generated QR card
- Action:
  - hold on the QR long enough for the audience to understand the phone handoff

### Shot 4 — Buyer route (0:30-0:46)

- Screen: viewer route on phone or second browser
- Actions:
  - connect wallet
  - click pay
  - pause while transaction confirms

### Shot 5 — Unlock + Explorer (0:46-0:58)

- Screen: unlocked content and Explorer link
- Actions:
  - briefly open Explorer confirmation
  - return to unlocked content

### Shot 6 — Access pass (0:58-1:10)

- Screen: access-pass card
- Actions:
  - mint access pass if it was not minted automatically
  - open NFT mint on Explorer

### Shot 7 — Close (1:10-1:20)

- Screen: access-pass art or landing page hero
- Voiceover:
  - This is not a platform. It is infrastructure.

## Recording notes

- Record one clean take locally before the venue.
- Keep both browser windows logged in and funded.
- Disable noisy notifications.
- If mobile capture is awkward, use a second desktop browser for the backup recording.
